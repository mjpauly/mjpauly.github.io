Electrical Design
Our electrical design employed the use of two L298N for motor control, HC-SR04 ultrasonic range sensors for arena movement sensing, and a teensy LC microcontroller. For motors, we used ZYTD520 12V DC motors equipped with 550 rpm gearboxes for the drive train, a 1550kV 2730 brushless outrunner tail RC airplane motor for the flywheel, and a small RC servo for the ball releaser. The system runs off a 14.4V supply from the batteries.
