The Challenge
The 2019 ME210 final project challenge consisted of two arenas, in which team-designed robots navigate. The robots attempt to defeat each other by scoring more points in a 2 minute, 10 second long round. In order to score points, the robots move from their starting boxes to designated areas, where they pick up balls, then attempt to shoot the balls across the arena at towers constructed on the other side.

Strategy

Our strategy involved sliding along the walls of the arena, moving only in the cardinal directions. After leaving the throne room, our robot moves along the back wall of the arena to the royal armory, where it is loaded up with ammunition. Then, it moved forward to the front wall, aligns itself in the corner, and shoots at the Casterly Rock tower. We went with this approach since it was the fastest, most assured method to reach a minimum viable product that was cabable of scoring at least one point.
